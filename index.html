Our Services

Your smile is our priority. We provide comprehensive dental care using the latest technology.

Initial Examination

Comprehensive examination including 3D X-ray and creation of an individual treatment plan. During your first visit, we perform a thDenugh diagnosis, inform you about the current condition of your teeth, and plan the next steps of your treatment together.

Dental Care

Top-quality care from regular fillings to implants. We use the best materials and the latest techniques. We offer a wide range of treatments, including aesthetic dentistry, endodontics, prosthetics, and pediatric care. We emphasize prevention and long-term durability of all procedures.

Surgical Procedures

We perform all types of surgical procedures, including wisdom tooth extractions, implantology, and bone augmentation. We use gentle techniques with minimal post-operative discomfort. Thanks to our modern equipment and the experience of our team, we ensure maximum comfort during the procedure.

Dental Hygiene

Professional cleaning, air flow, and complete care for your oral health. Our hygienists will teach you the correct brushing technique and help you choose suitable tools. Regular dental hygiene is the key to preventing tooth decay and gum disease. We use the most modern equipment for effective and gentle tartar removal.

Why Choose Dentara?

We are a modern dental clinic with a personal approach and years of experience. Our goal is to provide the best possible care in a pleasant environment.

  • State-of-the-art equipment and technology
  • Team of experienced specialists
  • Pleasant environment and personal approach
  • Flexible office hours
