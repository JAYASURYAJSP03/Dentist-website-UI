What can you expect during treatment?

01

Diagnosis & Planning

Thorough examination and X-rays to assess tooth health and plan the most conservative approach.

02

Tooth Preparation

Removal of only the damaged or decayed portion of the tooth, keeping as much natural structure as possible.

03

Restoration Placement

Application of high-quality composite, ceramic, or gold restorations for strength and aesthetics.

04

Final Adjustments

Shaping and polishing to ensure a perfect bite and natural appearance.

Why choose conservative dentistry?

Health

  • Preserves natural tooth structure
  • Prevents further damage
  • Minimizes need for future treatments

Appearance

  • Natural color matching
  • Smooth finish
  • Maintains natural smile

Aftercare & Prevention

Oral hygiene

Brush twice daily and floss to maintain restoration longevity.

Regular check-ups

Visit your dentist every 6 months to monitor restorations.

Healthy habits

Avoid biting hard objects and reduce sugary foods to protect your teeth.
